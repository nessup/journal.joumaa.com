When I was 15, a family friend asked me why I liked programming computers. It was hard to explain then, and to some extent still is hard to explain now, what initial exposure or set of experiences spurred my interest.

To better answer this question in the future, I've begun a list of works of art that I believe have significantly influenced my thinking about a few things. I hope others can do the same publicly so that we can self-reflect while learning about each other on the level of ideas, perhaps the most fundamental and effective way to understand each other in the context of our thoughts, cultures, and upbringings.
