When I was 15, a family friend asked me why I liked programming. It was hard to explain then, and to some extent still is hard to explain now, what initial exposure or set of experiences spurred my interest.

To better answer self-reflective questions like that in the future, I've begun a list of works that I believe have significantly influenced my thinking about things. I hope others can do the same publicly so that we can learn about each other on a level of ideas, perhaps the best way to understand each other in the context of our thoughts, cultures, and beliefs.
